Profile

My work combines geospatial analysis, remote sensing, and environmental interpretation to communicate complex spatial patterns in a clear and practical way. I have experience working with terrestrial and marine datasets, with projects spanning glacier retreat, wildfire regeneration, volcanic terrain change, forest resilience, and bathymetric analysis.

Alongside technical GIS skills, my background in social geography helps me think about how spatial data connects to land use, planning, environmental management, and decision-making.
