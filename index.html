Volcanic Change Detection

Mount St Helens terrain and lava dome change

Used remote sensing, DEMs, and classification workflows to analyse terrain loss from the eruption and glacier-lava dome dynamics over time.

Methods: DEM analysis, SVM classification, change quantification
Marine Survey Planning

Bathymetric survey design between Oʻahu and Kauaʻi

Assessed seabed depth, geomorphology, and sediment characteristics to support survey logistics, vessel selection, and a potential gas pipeline route.

Methods: Bathymetry, nautical chart interpretation, survey costing
Marine Archaeology

3D visualisation of historic shipwrecks

Combined bathymetric and side scan sonar data to create interpretable 3D wreck models for archaeological diving assessment and hazard review.

Methods: Side scan sonar, bathymetry, 3D modelling
Cryosphere Monitoring

Mýrdalsjökull glacier retreat and meltwater change

Investigated glacial retreat in Iceland across multiple years to quantify loss of ice and assess downstream implications for hydropower systems.

Methods: Glacier classification, retreat mapping, area comparison
Master’s Research

Boreal forest health, fungi networks, and soil moisture in Trysil

Mapped forest composition, ectomycorrhizal fungi associations, and soil moisture dynamics to explore climate resilience and forest regeneration patterns.

Methods: Multitemporal remote sensing, NDMI, change detection
Wildfire Recovery

Vegetation regeneration after wildfire in Engskogen, Sweden

Used Sentinel-based vegetation indices and SAR backscatter to analyse post-fire recovery and identify change in burned versus non-burned areas.

Methods: NDVI, MSAVI, SAR backscatter, change detection
